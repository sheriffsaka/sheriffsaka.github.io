About Us

Welcome to CEFN Kano

Few brethren of Ijala and idoma extractions came together to have a fellowship. They sought for an avenue where they could encourage themselves through the word of God.

They were conscious of their background and never wanted multiplicity of denomination to affect their values and standard.

Our Achievements

  • 1999

    First place of fellowship & Present

    They shared worship venues with other denomination until 1999 when they were privileged to come across GIDAN BISHARA. This Christian body accommodated the fellowship at No., 41 Warri Road, without any fees. To the glory of God, the church at Warri Road, bought the land and developed their new worship centre at 55 Abeokuta Road Sabon Gari, Kano State. The church displayed her commitment to the growth and development of Mission Zaria zone. It could not be outplace to say that her effort was acknowledge and rewarded by the fellowship headquarter by the creation of Kano Missionary Zone. This development impacted positively in the zone and led to the creation of a worship centre in Katsina. The unique role the two churches (Kano and Badawa) are playing have greatly empowered the zone to take some giant steps which you shall read more...

    Mission

    Our Mission

    To evangelize to all nations. To ensure the message of love of God is read in all parts of the world unhindered.

    Vision

    Our Vision to you

    To encourage fellow believers to be strong in the word and faith. Brethren living in freedom and purity conscious of the eminent living talk of Jesus Christ, the saviour of the whole world. People Prepare that seldomly cannot be renewed.
